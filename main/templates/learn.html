How to use Menpha

Menpha is an easy-to-use web app developed to solve a single task, and solve it well!


Menpha involves three easy and fast steps. You are more than welcome to execute these steps in any order of preference and as they are not sequential, you will still arrive at the same results.


-- Add your IMEI numbers of any device type you own (for instance, your smartphone) and indicate whether it is with you or not.


-- Search for any IMEI number and know whether its stolen or missing


-- You can choose to share the results with your friends when you learn an IMEI is no longer with the owner (in other words, stolen or missing).


We seek to serve you best by using these simple steps above so feel free to try it out. We hope you enjoy your stay with us.

What is IMEI number?

International Mobile Equipment Identity (IMEI), is used to identify a particular mobile phone. Every mobile phone comes with an IMEI and they're unique globally.


And because these IMEI are unique across all devices with them, Menpha employs that uniqueness in its reporting system, preventing a single IMEI from being added twice.


With this in mind, you can expect only a single result whenever you search against my database. If you do not come across a results of your search query, then it is likely the device is still in safe hands and you can proceed to purchase.
