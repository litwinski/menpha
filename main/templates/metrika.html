Step 1 - Sign Up and Login

Its time! Its free and instant, too. I only require your username, email and password in order to sign up. You will be asked to activate your account after signing up. Activate your account, and lets get going.

Step 2 - Search and Learn

Now's the fun part. Before you make a purchase a smartphone or any IMEI-tagged device, do well to check through our Database. Who knows, the device might be stolen. Now you know thus don't be tricked.
